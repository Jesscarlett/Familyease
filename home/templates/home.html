About Us


FamilyEase - your trusted partner for a hassle-free life. Based in North Shore and proudly rooted in the Chatswood community, FamilyEase was founded by me, a Corporate IT Manager with a passion for helping others and a desire to venture into small business.

Our mission is simple: to bring ease and peace of mind to families in the neighborhood. We understand the challenges of managing a busy household, especially during the holidays. That’s why we’re here to take care of the little things, so you can focus on creating memories, whether you're traveling, relaxing, or celebrating with loved ones.

Why trust us?

As a long-time Chatswood resident and a proud father of two beautiful daughters, I value community, family, and trust. With years of professional experience at one of the major banks, I’ve built my career on reliability and responsibility. These values are at the heart of everything we do. Let us be your friendly helping hand this holiday season. From minding your home to managing errands, we’re here to make your life easier.
